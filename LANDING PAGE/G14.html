About Vijay Thalapathy

Joseph Vijay is a celebrated Indian actor, singer and dancer! He is also known as Thalapathy (Commander) by his fans.

Who is Thalapathy Vijay

Bio

Thalapathy Vijay (born Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar on 22 June 1974) is an Indian actor, singer and dancer who works primarily in Tamil films. His successful films in Tamil cinema have earned him the title 'Thalapathy' (Commander), which has now become synonymous with his name. He is one of the highest-paid actors in the industry and has won several awards, including three Tamil Nadu State Film Awards and four Vijay Awards. He is also considered one of the most influential celebrities in India.

Early Life

Vijay was born to a Tamil family in Chennai. He is the son of film producer S. A. Chandrasekhar and playback singer Shoba Chandrasekhar. He attended Loyola College, Chennai, where he was actively involved in extra-curricular activities such as theatre and dancing. After college, he started his career as a child artist in films such as Vetri (1984), directed by his father.

MOVIES

Vijay made his debut as a lead actor in the 1992 film Naalaya Theerpu, directed by his father. The film was a commercial success and it established him as an actor in the Tamil film industry. He then acted in several films such as Poove Unakkaga (1996), Kadhalukku Mariyadhai (1997), Kushi (2000), Thamizhan (2002), Ghilli (2004), Sivakasi (2005) and Pokkiri (2007), which were all commercially successful ventures. He has since then gone on to star in several critically acclaimed and commercially successful films such as Thuppakki (2012), Kaththi (2014), Mersal (2017) and Sarkar (2018).

AWARDS

Vijay has won several awards throughout his career, including three Tamil Nadu State Film Awards for Best Actor, four Vijay Awards for Entertainer of the Year, two Filmfare Awards South for Best Actor - Tamil and two Edison Awards for Best Actor. In addition to this, he was also awarded the Kalaimamani Award by the Tamil Nadu Government in 2006.

Personal Life

Vijay married Sangeetha Sornalingam on 25 August 1999 and they have two children together: Jason Sanjay and Divya Saasha. He resides in Chennai with his family.
